We sometimes allow our farming neighbour to come onto our land to graze it down. When on the land the black metal gate (bottom of our field) will sometimes be left open and an electric pony tape may be strung across the gateway (to stop horses only). Generally the power to the fencing will be off and we would tell you if we decided to electrify
