*3.6 miles/5.8km | 2 hours |easy-moderate*

This short walk around Malham Tarn, beneath the looming cliffs of Great Close Scar, offers ramblers a chance to experience a variety of habitats, from craggy limestone bluffs, to thick forest, dark peatland and a wildlife-rich upland lake.
