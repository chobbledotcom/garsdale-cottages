When you first access this digital guidebook on your phone, follow the prompt to add it to your home screen. **This makes it:**

✅ faster 

✅ easier to access 

✅ available offline

Once on your phone it behaves just like any other app, but without you needing to download anything or sign in.

## For iPhone users

The "Add to Home Screen" only appears when using Safari browser. If Safari isn't your default browser, just copy the guidebook link and paste it into Safari. From there, you can follow the prompts to add the guidebook to your home screen.

## Always get the latest version of the guidebook

Our guidebook is regularly updated with information to help you get the most out of your stay. The app automatically updates every 24 hours. If you want to get the updates sooner, simply refresh the screen. To refresh the guide, swipe the guide closed, and tap the app icon again to fully reload.

## Share your guide with your group

You can send the link to this guidebook to everyone who’s travelling with you. That way, everyone has access to all the information they’ll need (which means less hassle for the trip organiser!).
