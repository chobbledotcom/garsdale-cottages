As LPG for the house has been discontinued you should have no need to consider any aspect of the system. The system is temporarily retained as a back up. If there is a concern, the LPG valve is located outside the back door, turn left up the steps and the valve is just on the left. Alternatively the LPG tanks can be isolated at source. The four upright cylinders are located beside the front door to the owners adjacent premises; Tarn Rigg Settle
